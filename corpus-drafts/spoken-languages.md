Alex is multilingual, working across English, Mandarin Chinese, and Cantonese in daily, professional, and technical contexts.

- **English — professional working proficiency.** His working language in Australia: software engineering and Salesforce development discussions, technical documentation, email, meeting summaries, interviews, job applications, and stakeholder communication all happen in English.
- **Mandarin Chinese (普通话) — native.** Native spoken and written Chinese, used frequently for reasoning, explanations, translation, technical clarification, and personal communication.
- **Cantonese (广东话 / 粤语) — native.** Native spoken language, used for everyday communication, cultural context, and Chinese-language interaction.

For teams working across English- and Chinese-speaking markets, he works comfortably in both — including translating and clarifying technical content between English and Chinese.
